## Account Access & Password Reset

If a customer cannot log in or has forgotten their password:

1. Go to the login page at caregateghana.com
2. Click the **"Forgot Password"** link below the login form
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. A password reset link will be sent to that email within 5 minutes
5. Check your spam/junk folder if the email does not appear in your inbox
6. Click the link in the email and follow the prompts to set a new password
7. If the reset email still does not arrive after 10 minutes, try again or contact support

The AI agent can fully resolve password reset and account access questions using the steps above.

## Billing Email & Invoice Management

**Updating billing email:**
- Log in to your account and go to **Account Settings → Profile → Billing Information**
- Update the billing email address and click Save
- Future invoices will be sent to the new address automatically

**Invoice not received:**
- Invoices are sent automatically to the billing email on file at the start of each billing cycle
- If you did not receive an invoice, first check that your billing email address is correct in Account Settings
- You can also download all past invoices from **Account Settings → Billing → Invoice History**
- Check your spam/junk folder, as automated emails sometimes get filtered

The AI agent can resolve billing email and invoice delivery questions using the above steps.

## Refund Policy

CareGate Ghana offers a **30-day money-back guarantee** on all subscription plans.

To request a refund:
- Contact support within 30 days of your original purchase date
- Provide your order number or the email address used at checkout
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method

Refund requests within the 30-day window with a valid order number can be acknowledged and confirmed by the AI agent.

Escalate to a human agent if:
- The refund request is outside the 30-day window
- The customer disputes a chargeback
- There are signs of fraud or account compromise

## Technical Issues & App Troubleshooting

**App crashing or not loading:**
- Ensure your device software is up to date
- Uninstall and reinstall the app from the official app store
- Clear the app cache (Android: Settings → Apps → CareGate → Clear Cache)
- Try logging in on a different device or browser to isolate the issue
- If the issue persists, share your device model, OS version, and a description of the error so our technical team can investigate

For persistent crashes or errors that do not resolve with the above steps, escalate to a human agent with the customer's device details.

## Escalation Rules

Always hand off to a human agent when:
- The customer makes a legal threat or mentions a lawyer
- The issue involves a chargeback or disputed bank transaction
- The customer reports potential account compromise or unauthorized access
- The required action needs access to the customer's specific account data that is not self-service
